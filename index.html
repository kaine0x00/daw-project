About me!

Hi, I'm Danny Robinson but my friends call me Kainé. I'm a self-taught programmer and an all-around computer nerd. I also love music, science fiction, cats, video games and coffee.
Throughout my trajectory I have gotten familiar with quite a few programming languages such as: Python, C and C++, Golang, Rust, JS, C# and Java.
Cybersecurity is one of my favourite aspects of IT, i just love hacking things, with permission of course :)
I really enjoy the idea of combining programming with ethical hacking, developing my own tools and using my coding knowledge to both make and break all sorts of different software.
If I have to choose between backend and frontend, I would go for backend any day of the week. Low level programming, reverse engineering, malware development and gamedev are my cup of tea.
